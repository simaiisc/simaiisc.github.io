About Us

Founded in the '70s, Science Institute Malayali Association (SIMA) is the official forum of the Malayalam speaking community in Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. SIMA is a broad umbrella under which we endeavour to imbibe the spirit of togetherness with which we are familiar in our state, Kerala. SIMA draws its strength from the able hands of the Secretary, the executive committee constituted by the student members and the President and vice-President, who are faculty representatives. However, it owes its glorious existence to the active participation by all the members in various activities all round the year.

We, the SIMA family, get together at various times of the year to create and cherish a few fond memories. The occasions include the celebration of festivals like Onam, Christmas etc. , the welcoming of freshers into the family & the charity drives to name a few. Whatever might be the event, there has always been a zeal on the part of its members to participate with full vigour. With a lot of events to look forward to each year, SIMA endeavours to make sure that we make our presence felt in diverse areas such as social welfare, sports and arts. In short, SIMA at IISc is a home away from home in all sense of the word.
